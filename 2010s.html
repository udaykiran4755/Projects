2010s: The Smart Shift

Apps, the cloud, and AI assistants went mainstream, putting an intelligent, connected world in our hands.

Instagram (2010)

Instagram (2010)

This photo-sharing app defined mobile-first social media. Its filters and square format changed photography and marketing forever.

Voice Assistants (2011)

Voice Assistants (2011)

Apple's Siri launched on the iPhone 4S, kicking off the race for AI-powered assistants like Alexa and Google Assistant.

Cloud Services (2011)

Cloud Services (2011)

With services like iCloud and Google Drive, the "cloud" went mainstream, syncing our files, photos, and data across all devices.

Smartwatches

Smartwatches

Devices like the Pebble watch, Apple Watch, and Samsung Galaxy Gear became popular, incorporating features like fitness tracking, heart rate monitors, and even EKG readings.

Streaming Wars (2013)

Streaming Wars (2013)

Netflix released "House of Cards," its first original series. This proved streaming wasn't just a library, but a content creator, starting the war for subscribers.
