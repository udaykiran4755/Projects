2020s: The AI-Powered World

Generative AI, new realities, and decentralized tech begin to reshape our world at an exponential pace.

Generative AI (2022)

Models like ChatGPT and DALL-E 2 went public, allowing anyone to create human-like text, code, and stunning images from simple prompts.

AR & VR (2023)

With powerful, standalone headsets like the Quest 3 and Apple's Vision Pro, mixed and virtual reality moved from niche gaming to spatial computing.

Decentralized Web (2021)

Concepts like Web3, DAOs, and NFTs exploded, proposing a new internet built on blockchain, owned by users, not corporations.

AI in Science (2021)

DeepMind's AlphaFold2 solved the 50-year-old "protein folding problem," a breakthrough in biology that could accelerate drug discovery.

mRNA Vaccines (2020)

A new type of vaccine, developed at record speed to combat COVID-19, proving the power of computational biology and bio-tech.
